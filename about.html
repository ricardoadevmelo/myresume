About Me

Hello, I'm Ricardo Alves Melo, a technology enthusiast with a passion for continuous learning. I’m currently improving my English at Icot College and deepening my knowledge in Systems Analysis and Development, focusing on front-end development as I pursue a career transition into tech. My professional journey has been diverse and enriching. Over the years, I’ve worked as a salesperson, sales representative, and store manager in construction materials and agricultural products. I also practiced law and served in the public sector, working as an advisor and judge assistant at the Brazilian Court of Justice. Today, my path has led me to Ireland, where I work in customer service in Cork, a vibrant city that has become my home.

My Interests
Beyond technology, I have a deep love for outdoor activities and sports that challenge me. Weight training, rappelling, sandboarding, snowboarding, and surfing are part of my lifestyle. I also enjoy exploring new cultures, visiting historical sites, and traveling to new destinations. My journey has already taken me to incredible places like Norway, Sweden, Scotland, and now, I'm experiencing life in Cork, Ireland. Whether in the pursuit of new professional challenges or exploring the world around me, I'm always moving forward, learning, and evolving.
